Use Case 1: Inventory Reorder Calculator

📦 საწყობის მენეჯერებისთვის

ChatGPT Prompt: "შექმენი Google Sheets formula: მონაცემები: Column A: Product Name Column B: Current Stock Column C: Average Daily Sales (last 30 days) Column D: Lead Time (days from supplier) Column E: Safety Stock Level (days) გამოთვალე: Column F: Reorder Point Column G: Optimal Order Quantity Column H: Status (OK / Low / Critical / Overstock) Status logic: - Critical: stock < 3 days - Low: stock < reorder point - OK: adequate stock - Overstock: stock > 60 days მომეცი ზუსტი formula თითოეული სვეტისთვის"
AI მოგცემს Formulas:

F2: =D2*C2+E2*C2
(Reorder Point = Lead Time × Daily Sales + Safety Stock × Daily Sales)

G2: =SQRT(2*C2*30*50/2)
(Economic Order Quantity - simplified)

H2: =IF(B2C2*60,"Overstock","OK")))
(Status based on conditions)

დროის დაზოგვა: Manual calculation-ი: 2-3 საათი კვირაში
AI-ით: 10 წუთი setup + ავტომატური განახლება = 95% დაზოგვა!

⚙️ საოპერაციო მენეჯერებისთვის

ChatGPT Prompt: "შექმენი detailed SOP document: Process: [process name] Department: Gastronome.ge [specify area] Who performs: [role] Frequency: [daily/weekly/as needed] Critical for: [quality/safety/compliance/efficiency] SOP Structure: 1. Purpose (2 sentences - why this process matters) 2. Scope (who, when, where this applies) 3. Step-by-Step Procedure - Numbered steps with clear actions - Sub-steps where needed - Estimated time for each major step 4. Quality Checkpoints - What to verify at key stages - Acceptance criteria 5. Common Mistakes to Avoid - 3-5 frequent errors and how to prevent 6. Troubleshooting - If X happens, do Y 7. Escalation Process - When to call manager/supervisor - Who to contact for specific issues 8. Related Documents & Tools - Links to forms, checklists, systems Format: - Max 2 pages - Visual-friendly (leave space for photos/diagrams) - Clear, action-oriented language - Checkbox format where applicable"
