## 3. Curriculum — 8 modules, 21 hours (Tier B recommended)

Each module = 2.5h live + 0.5h async homework. Sessions mix theory (40%), guided practice (40%), and team exercises (20%). All modules use the **CREATE prompt framework** (Context, Role, Exact task, Audience, Template, Examples) as the spine.

| # | Module | Hours | Key output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **AI foundations & safe prompting** | 2.5h | Personal CREATE prompt library, 5 vetted use cases |
| 2 | **Email, meeting & professional comms** | 2.5h | Email templates, meeting-minutes pipeline, DEFUSE framework |
| 3 | **SOPs, slide generation, visual ops** | 2.5h | 3 working SOPs, 1 AI-generated presentation |
| 4 | **Sales enablement, objection handling** | 2.5h | BANT qualification script, 14-day nurture sequence |
| 5 | **Excel, data analysis, inventory routes** | 2.5h | 5 working formulas, 1 ROP-style analysis |
| 6 | **Personal AI assistants & Deep Research** | 2.5h | 1 working Custom GPT/Gem, 1 NotebookLM knowledge base |
| 7 | **No-code automation (n8n, Make.com, Lovable)** | 2.5h | 1 live workflow + 1 internal mini-tool |
| 8 | **Company AI policy, GDPR, security** | 2.5h | Draft AI policy, PII checklist, 30-day rollout roadmap |

### Why these 8, not 10 or 6

- **6 is too thin** to cover both operational comms AND policy — clients who skip policy regret it in month 4.
- **10+ dilutes** the 21h into surface-level exposure. The 8-module structure maps to the standard offer-builder pattern (Tempo Obsidian, GIPA, Orbi, Ardi) and has been validated across cohorts.
