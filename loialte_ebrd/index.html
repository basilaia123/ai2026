Why Cat B is the structural backbone

Nima uchun Cat B strukturaviy tayanch

EBRD AI Capacity-Building RFIs split into three lots. Without Cat B, the executive awareness (Lot A) and developer track (Lot C) have nothing to land on — managers approve budgets for tools their staff cannot use.

The 8-module curriculum

8-მოდულიანი პროგრამა

8 modulli dastur

All modules use the CREATE framework (Context, Role, Exact task, Audience, Template, Examples) as the prompt-engineering spine.

ყველა მოდული CREATE ჩარჩოს ეფუძნება.

Barcha modullar CREATE tizimidan foydalanadi (Kontekst, Rol, Aniq vazifa, Auditoriya, Shablon, Misollar).
