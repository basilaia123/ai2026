## Branding Guidelines (Tempo Holding)
- **Name:** Always use "Tempo Holding" or "Tempo".
- **Tone:** Professional, elite, yet accessible. Avoid overly robotic or dry language.
- **Responsibility:** When addressing delays or issues, use "თავის მართლების გარეშე" (without making excuses) and focus on "პასუხისმგებლობის აღება" (taking responsibility).
- **Quality:** Language must be high-quality Georgian (FiraGO font is standard for web).

## Grammar & Style Rules
1. **Verbs:** Ensure correct conjugation for formal business contexts.
2. **Punctuation:** Use standard Georgian punctuation rules.
3. **Naturalness:** Prefer natural Georgian phrasing over literal translations of English idioms.
4. **Lists:** Replace "ბულეტები" with "პუნქტები" or "პუნქტობრივი ჩამონათვალი".
5. **UI Mocks:** Even in visual email mockups, translate "Subject", "Body", and "CTA" to Georgian for a localized premium feel.
