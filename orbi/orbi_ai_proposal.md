# AI & Automation Training Proposal - ORBI Group (Central Park Towers)
## Integrated Operations Program for Construction, Engineering, Design, and Project Management

---

### 🏢 Project Context & Research (Central Park Towers)

**Central Park Towers** is one of the most prestigious and large-scale multifunctional projects (integrated resort) in the region, developed by **ORBI Group** in partnership with **Block Group** on Alexander Kazbegi Avenue, Tbilisi.

* **Scale:** 140,000 m² of total space, two 42-story towers, and a 6-story podium.
* **Infrastructure:** International hotel brands (Radisson Blu, Planet Hollywood), the region's largest spa and wellness center, a 4,000-seat concert/event hall, a premium shopping gallery (e.g., Harvey Nichols retail spaces), a casino, a Michelin-starred restaurant on the top floor, and a 25,000 m² automated smart parking system.
* **Target Audience (15-20 Participants):** Managers and specialists from 6 key departments:
  1. **Plumbing Manager & Team** (water supply, sewage, fire hydrants, spa/wellness engineering).
  2. **Electrical Manager & Team** (high/low voltage networks, lighting scenarios, generator grids, automated system power).
  3. **Architecture Team** (exterior, BIM/CAD modeling, spatial planning, local regulations).
  4. **Design Team** (interior layouts, material mood boards, furniture, hotel/casino aesthetics).
  5. **Project Manager & Coordinators** (scheduling, budgeting, contractor coordination, risk registers).
  6. **IT Manager & Team** (BMS/Smart Building systems, automated parking APIs, networking, data security).

### 🛠️ Core AI & No-Code Toolkit (Software Focus)

The training will focus on five industry-leading tools, demonstrating their direct application to Central Park Towers' operations:

1. **Generative AI Engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini):** Used for advanced prompting, contract analysis, drafting safety procedures, writing automation scripts (PowerShell, AutoCAD LISP), and creating custom GPTs/Claude Projects tailored to MEP compliance.
2. **Google NotebookLM:** A localized project wiki. Uploading equipment specs, local design codes, and hotel brand manuals to create a private Q&A system and automated audio summaries.
3. **Notion & Notion AI:** Serving as the central database backend. Relational tracking of tasks, material schedules, and contractor lists across all departments.
4. **Lovable.app:** An advanced AI-powered full-stack app builder. Used by architects, designers, PMs, and IT to build and iterate on interactive web dashboards, defect reporting interfaces, and project portals using natural language.
5. **Make.com:** The integration engine. Connecting Lovable.app forms and Notion databases with corporate communications (Outlook, Slack) for automated status changes and notification alerts.

### 📅 Session Schedule (Option B: 12 Hours / 4 Days - Recommended)

Every session is delivered in person. The 3-hour daily meetings are divided into two focused work blocks and a coffee break.

#### Day 1: AI Foundations, Security, and Universal Prompting (3 Hours)
* **Block 1 (1 hour 25 minutes):**
  * Introduction to ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini in construction, engineering, and real estate.
  * **Data Privacy and Security:** Rules for handling proprietary architectural drawings, budgets, and schedules without leaking them to public AI training databases.
* **☕ Coffee Break (10 minutes)**
* **Block 2 (1 hour 25 minutes):**
  * **The Universal Prompt Framework:** Mastering Role, Context, Task, Format, Tone, and Constraints.
  * Administrative productivity: automating official correspondence, writing meeting summaries, and summarizing technical reports.
* **📋 Practical Assignment:** Select a routine administrative task and build a reusable Universal Prompt to automate it.

#### Day 2: Prompt Engineering, Custom Assistants, and NotebookLM (3 Hours)
* **Block 1 (1 hour 25 minutes):**
  * **Advanced Prompting:** Few-shot prompting, roleplay modeling, and parsing technical documents.
  * **Building Custom Assistants:** Setting up Custom GPTs and Claude Projects. Creating dedicated assistants for Central Park Towers (e.g., MEP Standard Compliance Checkers, Tender Specification Reviewers).
* **☕ Coffee Break (10 minutes)**
* **Block 2 (1 hour 25 minutes):**
  * **NotebookLM in Practice:** Using Google's NotebookLM to manage project documentation. Uploading datasheets, hotel standards, and manuals.
  * Navigating the project database, running Q&A, and generating structured Audio Overviews (AI podcasts) summarizing complex specs.
* **📋 Practical Assignment:** Create a NotebookLM project with 2-3 technical datasheets and draft a custom assistant prototype in Claude Projects.

#### Day 3: Notion & Lovable.app Prototyping (3 Hours)
* **Block 1 (1 hour 25 minutes):**
  * **Notion Databases as a Backend:** Pages, databases, Relations, and Rollups. Building a structured data scheme for Central Park Towers.
  * **Notion AI:** Summarizing pages and extracting action items from meeting notes.
* **☕ Coffee Break (10 minutes)**
* **Block 2 (1 hour 25 minutes):**
  * **Lovable.app No-Code Development:** Introduction to Lovable.app for rapid web app prototyping.
  * Designing a custom web interface (e.g., a "Project Dashboard" or a "Site Defect Reporter") that connects to databases.
* **📋 Practical Assignment:** Create a Notion database structure and prototype a basic web app front-end in Lovable.app.

#### Day 4: Make.com - Workflow Automation (3 Hours)
* **Block 1 (1 hour 25 minutes):**
  * **Make.com Core Concepts:** Scenarios, Triggers, Actions, and Routers (No-Code automation).
  * Connecting Notion and Lovable.app to Make.com: updating project databases automatically.
* **☕ Coffee Break (10 minutes)**
* **Block 2 (1 hour 25 minutes):**
  * Building practical automations: Syncing Outlook emails to Notion/Lovable task boards.
  * Setting up Slack/email alerts triggered by portal status updates.
  * Integrating AI modules inside Make.com scenarios for automatic email analysis and response drafting.
* **📋 Practical Assignment:** Create a live Make.com scenario that automatically creates a Notion entry when a specific email trigger is received.
